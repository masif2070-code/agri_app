Privacy Policy for Agrology

Effective Date: April 8, 2026

Agrology is an agriculture advisory app that provides crop guidance, weather and irrigation support, livestock/pet guidance, commodity indicators, and photo-based recommendations for crop and animal symptoms. This Privacy Policy explains what data we process, why we process it, and your choices.

1. Data We Process

1.1 Location Data (Optional)

  • Precise or approximate location only when you grant permission and use location-based features.
  • Used for weather display, province detection, and map/field analysis features.

1.2 Crop and Animal Advisory Inputs

  • Selected crop, growth stage, irrigation count, and optional map boundary (field polygon).
  • Selected animal type, symptom focus, and symptom notes.

1.3 Photos You Choose to Upload

  • Crop photos for crop recommendation requests.
  • Animal photos for animal symptom recommendation requests.
  • Photos are uploaded only when you press the recommendation submit button.

1.4 Basic Technical Data

  • Request metadata and service logs needed for security, troubleshooting, and reliability.
  • No ads profile or marketing profile is built by the app.

2. How We Use Data

  • Deliver core app functions (weather, crop/animal recommendations, map analysis).
  • Process user-requested photo analysis for advisory outputs.
  • Maintain service stability, prevent abuse, and debug operational issues.

We do not sell personal data.

3. Data Sharing and Processors

Data is shared only as required for app functionality:

  • Agrology backend for advisory processing: https://agri-app-backend-6kyx.onrender.com
  • Weather provider (Open-Meteo) for weather forecasts using location coordinates.
  • Mapping and geospatial services (for map tiles and optional satellite-related features).
  • News and market reference feeds used for headline and commodity indicators.

Third-party providers have their own privacy policies and terms.

4. Data Retention

  • Most recommendation inputs are processed per request and are not used to create long-term user profiles.
  • Operational logs may be retained for security and reliability according to hosting provider policy.
  • In-app state is primarily local and can be removed by clearing app data or uninstalling the app.

5. Your Choices

  • You can deny location permission and still use non-location features.
  • You can choose not to upload crop or animal photos.
  • You can remove local app data from your device settings at any time.

6. Security

  • Data in transit is sent over HTTPS.
  • Access to backend systems is restricted to authorized service components.
  • No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we apply reasonable safeguards.

7. Children

The app is intended for general users and not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us so we can review and address it.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always show an updated effective date.
